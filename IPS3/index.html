YASHVANTH AUDI Ltd.

Yashvanth, the dynamic owner of Audi, introduced the brand in 2005, marking the beginning of a new era in the automotive world. With a relentless drive for perfection, he quickly turned Audi into a global powerhouse, known for its precision engineering and unmatched performance. As Audi's success grew, Yashvanth's vision expanded. He sought to create an affordable yet high-speed vehicle capable of reaching an astonishing 320 km/h. His innovative approach led him to transform police cars into high-performance Audi models, enabling law enforcement to catch criminals with unparalleled speed and efficiency..
